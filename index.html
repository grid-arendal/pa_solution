The Van Long Nature Reserve is approximately 50 kilometers south of the Vietnamese capital of Hanoi. It is home to a critically endangered species of primate known as the Delacourâ€™s langur.

The purpose of this interactive map is to support decision making and management of the existing Van Long nature reserve and its surrounding areas. Different data layers are already available for this map, and more will be added until the end of the project (January 2019). Metadata is available for each layer, and data layers can also be downloaded directly from this website.

This interactive map is being developed by GRID-Arendal as part of the Protected Area Solutions project which is led by the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN).

Please use the Contact Us tab to reach out for any inquiries about the project or the interactive data viewer.
